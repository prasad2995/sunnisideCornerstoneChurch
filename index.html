Who We Are

Built on the Cornerstone

At Sunniside Cornerstone Church, our name reflects our conviction: Christ is the cornerstone on which everything is built. We are a Bible-believing, Spirit-filled congregation committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.

We welcome people from all walks of life — families, singles, young and old — because we believe the Gospel is for everyone. Our heart is to see lives transformed by the power of Jesus Christ.

✝ Authentic Worship

We encounter God through heartfelt, Spirit-led worship each week.

📖 Biblical Teaching

God's Word is our foundation and guide for all of life.

🤝 Community

We do life together — supporting, encouraging, and loving one another.

🌍 Outreach

We are called to share the good news with our neighbourhood and beyond.
